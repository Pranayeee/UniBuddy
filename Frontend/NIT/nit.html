National Institutes of Technology (NITs)

The National Institutes of Technology (NITs) are a group of premier public engineering institutions in India. They are governed by the National Institutes of Technology, Science Education, and Research Act, 2007, which declares them as institutions of national importance. There are 31 NITs spread across India, each offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering and technology disciplines. NITs are known for their rigorous academic curriculum, excellent faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Admission to NITs is primarily through the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main

NIT Bhopal

Offers a wide range of engineering courses with strong placements in software and core industries.

NIT Warangal

Founded in 1959, it is one of the oldest NITs and excels in research and technology development.

NIT Nagpur

Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT), Nagpur, is one of the premier engineering institutions in India. Established in 1960, it was formerly known as Visvesvaraya Regional College of Engineering (VRCE). VNIT is named after the eminent engineer and statesman, Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya. The institute is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India.

NIT Durgapur

Located in West Bengal, it has a strong placement record and a growing research ecosystem.

NIT Jamshedpur

Situated in the industrial hub, it offers great exposure to core engineering sectors.

NIT Srinagar

One of the oldest NITs, playing a vital role in technological advancements in the region.

NIT Surathkal

Established in 1960, NITK is situated in Mangalore, Karnataka, and is renowned for its excellent academic programs and research in engineering. It has strong industry connections, especially in computer science, civil, and mechanical engineering, and boasts a stunning coastal campus with a strong reputation for its placement record. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering and technology disciplines.

NIT Surat

Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT) Surat is one of the premier NITs in India, specializing in civil, mechanical, and computer engineering. It has strong industry collaborations and excellent placement records.

NIT Calicut

Known for its excellent faculty, infrastructure, and strong industry collaborations.

NIT Allahabad

A premier institute specializing in IT, electronics, and mechanical engineering disciplines.

NIT Rourkela

Recognized for its research contributions, diverse student community, and vibrant campus life.

NIT Jaipur

A well-reputed institute in Rajasthan known for its technical expertise and strong alumni network.

NIT Tiruchirappalli

Established in 1964, NIT Trichy (also known as National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli) is one of the oldest and top-ranking NITs in India. Located in Tamil Nadu, it is recognized for its high academic standards, exceptional research output, and excellent placement records.

NIT Kurukshetra

Established in 1963, it has strong industry ties and is known for its robust curriculum.

NIT Silchar

A fast-growing institute with a focus on infrastructure, research, and international collaborations.

NIT Hamirpur

Located in Himachal Pradesh, it is known for its scenic campus and focus on sustainable engineering.

NIT Patna

One of the oldest technical institutes in India, known for its rapidly improving academics and research.

NIT Jalandhar

A well-ranked NIT with strong programs in computer science and mechanical engineering.

NIT Raipur

Offers strong programs in architecture, biomedical, and traditional engineering streams.

NIT Agartala

A growing NIT with a focus on energy research, civil engineering, and environmental sciences.

NIT Delhi

Located in the capital city, it offers exposure to top industries and government organizations.

NIT Arunachal Pradesh

Established in 2010, it emphasizes sustainable development and energy research.

NIT Goa

Well-regarded for its faculty and research in computer science, data analytics, and AI.

NIT Manipur

Offers strong technical education with increasing collaborations with industries.

NIT Meghalaya

A young NIT focusing on research-driven education and digital learning methodologies.

NIT Mizoram

One of the newest NITs, developing strong research facilities in electronics and IT.

NIT Puducherry

Known for its student-centric teaching and expanding research facilities.

NIT Nagaland

A developing institute with a focus on rural technology, electrical, and computer science.

NIT Sikkim

Growing rapidly with an emphasis on AI, machine learning, and cybersecurity courses.

NIT Uttarakhand

Focused on renewable energy and environmental sustainability projects.

NIT Andhra Pradesh

A new-generation NIT with a focus on IoT, robotics, and industrial automation.
